Associate a did:key with your ATproto account

Paste a did:key:... string or scan a QR code containing one. A com.publicdomainrelay.temp.badgeBlueKeys record is created on your PDS linking the key to your account.

Share an association link by generating a QR code for any listed key. Others scan it, land here, confirm, and associate the same key with their own account.

The service field scopes the key to a specific service:

  • * — key valid for all services (default).
  • my-service — key valid only for that service.

After creation, the record is visible at pdsls.dev.
